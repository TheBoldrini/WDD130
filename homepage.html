PGL Major - Antwerp 2022

PGL Major Antwerp 2022 Format

Challengers Stage

The Challengers Stage (May 9-12) has 16 teams, which are in the “Contenders and Challengers” position.
This stage will adopt the Swiss format, in which each team faces another that has the same balance of wins and losses.
The series are in MD1 (best of 1), with the exception of playoffs and eliminations, which take place in MD3.

The top eight teams from the Challengers Stage advance to Legends, while the other eight are eliminated.

Legends Stage

At the Legends Stage (May 14-17) of the PGL Major Antwerp 2022, eight teams arrive from the previous stage and join eight previously ranked teams. Here, the Swiss format with MD1 and MD3 series is also used in case of decisive or knockout matches.

The top eight teams advance to the Champions Stage of the PGL Major Antwerp 2022.

Champions Stage

The Champions Stage (May 19-22) is nothing more than the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 playoffs. The eight Legends teams competed in a single elimination format, meaning whoever loses is out. All series are in MD3.
